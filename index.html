AR Portal

The original intention of augmented reality is to change what we see. It aims to enhance our experience and increase our happiness while exploring the world. Therefore, AR is an ideal tool for tourism. It adds new value and brings new opportunities to tourists and the tourism industry. Exploring tourist destinations and attractions through augmented reality can make those classic city trips even more extraordinary. Thus, this project is to develop an application for people have the difficulty of not being able to travel. Through this application, users can easily access another places, even if the user is in their home.
